About me


I live and work in the Chattanooga, TN area. I'm a graduate of the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where I received my master's degree in Computer Science. I'm fortunate enough to make a living creating software which empowers people in their daily lives and saves businesses time and money. I've been an adjunct at UTC and enjoy teaching and mentoring in general. I have a passion for teaching and am committed to lifelong learning. Some of my passions include playing and developing video games, wushu, taiji, xing yi, weight lifting and creating electronic music.

When I'm not working on di's projects from my office in Lakesite, TN I'm probably spending time with my two cats or training at the gym.

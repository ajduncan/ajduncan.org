About me


Born and raised in the Chattanooga, TN area. I'm a graduate of the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where I received my Master's degree in Computer Science. I've taught an introduction course in computer science. I have a passion for learning, teaching, sharing and firmly believe that lifelong learning has the potential to improve our lives. I'm fortunate enough to make a living using my abilities to create software which enriches quality of life and empowers people in their daily lives. My hobbies include playing and developing video games, gardening, tai-chi and creating electronic music.

When I'm not working out of di's offices in Hixson, TN I'm probably spending time with my wife, Melissa and our bengal cat, Chibi.

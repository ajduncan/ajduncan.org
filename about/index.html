Andrew "Andy" Duncan


Born and raised in the Chattanooga, TN area. I'm a graduate of the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga, where I received a Masters in Computer Science. I've taught an introduction course and enjoy teaching, sharing and education in general. My hobbies include playing and developing video games, gardening, tai-chi and electronic music.

When I'm not working out of d.i.'s offices in Hixson, TN I'm probably spending time with my wife, Melissa and our bengal cat, Chibi.
